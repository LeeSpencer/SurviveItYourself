About Us

This site was created by Spencer Lee and John Nulf. Spencer and John are students at Charlotte High School and members of the student organization Business Professionals of America (BPA). For the 2016-2017 school year, they entered a "Website Deisgn Team" competition. The criteria was to create a DIY (Do it yourself) website, and wanting to escape the dainty crafts and recipies of stereotypical DIY websites, they opted for something a little more hardcore. Thus, Survive It Yourself was born!
