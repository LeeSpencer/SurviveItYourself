How to Build a Water Filter

Supplies You'll Need:

  • Bottle
  • Cotton Balls
  • Activated Carbon (Charcoal)
  • Gravel
  • Sand
  1. Make the casing

    2. First, you will need something to put your magic filtering ingredients in. That's what the bottle is for. We used a 2-Liter bottle, but it can be any size depending on how much water you want to filter at a time.

  2. Create the lining

    3. Next, take one or two cotton balls, and shove them into the bottleneck. This is the end that the water will pour through.

  3. [Optional]Keep the lining in place

    4. If you're having trouble keeping the cotton balls from falling out, you can take the cap for the bottle and cut/poke/drill a hole (about a quarter inch) and screw it on loosely.
